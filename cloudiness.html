Cloudiness

Cloudiness does not display a large, discernable trend when compared with latitude. Locations from -40 to 70 degrees latitude all show a wide variety of cloudiness ranging from a 5-day average of 0% cloud cover to a 5-day average of over 90% cloud cover. Cloudiness is likely more directly influenced by other factors like time of day or proximity of a body of water.
