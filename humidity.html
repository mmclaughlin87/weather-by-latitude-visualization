Humidity

There does not appear to be a strong, discernable trend between latitude and humidity. Of interesting note, many of the locations used in this study appear to have very high humidity, with many averaging 100% humidity. This may have been influenced by the selection of cities closest to a random set of geocoordinates, which favored coastal cities.
